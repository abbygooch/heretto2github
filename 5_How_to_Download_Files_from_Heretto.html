How to Download Files from Heretto

You have all of your DITA content in Heretto, so now it's time to upload it to Github. The first step to this is downloading your DITA files to your computer. Keep reading to learn how.

  1. Open your DITA map.
    Since your DITA map displays all of your content, now would be a good time to do a double check that all of your files have been added to the map and ensure all of your DITA looks exactly how you want it to.
  2. Click "Publish" in the top right corner of the screen.
  3. From the list of publication options, choose "html5-bootstrap (blue)".
    Once you select your publication option, you can enter a short description for your publication if desired. Ensure that the "Navigate to output list" box is checked at the bottom of the dialog box so you can download your published files.
  4. Click "Publish" in the bottom right corner of the dialog box to confirm your choices.
  5. You will be automatically directed to the output list, where you can watch your publication in progress. It may take a few minutes to publish.
  6. Once completed, your publication will appear in the "Finished" column. Click your publication for more information about it.
  7. Click the "Download" button in the top right corner of the publication dialog box.
    Your files will download as a ZIP file that must be unzipped to be used. To unzip your folder, simply locate it in your computer files and double click the file name.
Your files will be immediately downloaded to your computer!
