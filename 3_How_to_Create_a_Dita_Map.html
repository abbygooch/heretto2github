How to Create a DITA Map

The DITA map will provide a structure for your content, and will link all of your individual files to one homepage. This task will guide you through the process of creating a map.

  1. Click the "Create New" button in the top left corner of the screen.
    Again, ensure new files you create are inside of your project folder.
  2. Choose "Map" from the "Map Types" section of the list.
  3. Name your map "document".
    For reference, the full file name of your map should be "document.ditamap".
  4. Click "Create" to save your DITA map.
  5. To add content to your DITA map, double click on the file to open the editor.
  6. In the Heretto editor, click "Open Library" to add files from your library to your DITA map.
  7. Drag files directly from your folder into your DITA map.
    You can add your index page now, and repeat this process to add the rest of your DITA files as you create them.
  8. Once your files are added to your map, click the "Close Library" button in the top right corner of the screen to view your map.
  9. You will now see all of your DITA content on one page: the map.
    You can edit your DITA content within the map once its created, or within the original files. All changes made on the DITA map will be reflected in the individual files, and vice-versa.
  10. Once satisfied with your edits, click the X in top right corner to close the editor.
Your DITA map has now been created!
