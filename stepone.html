How to Create a New Folder

The first step to creating content on Heretto to be uploaded to GitHub is to create a new folder for your content. This task will guide you through the steps of creating a folder.

  1. Open Heretto.
  2. Click the folder icon in the upper right corner of the screen. When you hover over the icon with your mouse, you will see it says "Create a new folder".
  3. Give the folder a name.
    Make sure your folder name is memorable and has no spaces.
  4. Click "Create" to create your folder.
Your new folder has been created!
